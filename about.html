About Me

“Hello there, my name is Kedinuwa Okuedei and I am extremely passionate about our community - Ukwuani LGA

They say give a man a fish and you feed him for a day, but teach him to fish and you feed him forever. I know that Ukwuani people are very hardworking and with the right opportunities and infrastructure, they’ll grow their businesses, families & communities.

I refuse to accept that our economy and our democracy are too broken to fix. For the sake of our children, we must build opportunity for every person in Ukwuani LGA and restore integrity to our government.

I believe that it’s time to change the political narrative and I’m running for House of Assembly to make sure you have the support and infrastructure you need to grow today & secure your future."

Meet Kedinuwa Okwuedei

Kedi was born 9th October 1985 to a family of 15 under the parenting of Mr & Mrs Bright Okuedei in Obiaruku. He attended Anointed Group of Schools, Obiaruku and St. Patrick College, Asaba for his primary and secondary school education. He then proceeded to Delta State University, Abraka for his university education where he studied Industrial Mathematics. Upon graduation, he moved to the United Kingdom to further his education, first in Business Study at London School of Business and Management and then on to the University of Wales, Newport for his Masters in Business Administration (MBA).

He returned to Nigeria to start up his business Pack-a-go; a logistic company focused on helping SMEs meet their business objectives. By providing quick, convenient and affordable logistics service to small businesses, Kedi's startup has empowered SMEs to expand their reach while achieving their business and revenue goals.

He's married to Josephine Okuedei, blessed with two sons Somfechukwu and Ifechukwuyenum and together they support the Ukwuani community through the Kedinuwa Foundation which focuses on intervention and empowerment for members of the Ukwuani community.

Driven by an obligation to create more opportunity for the next generation, he's taking his passion one step further in 2023 by running for House of Assembly as Representative for Ukwuani LGA.

  • our Campaign Promise

We believe strongly in empowerment

We will expand opportunity for all because empowered communities thrive and help empower other people. Our single focus will be on how to strengthen our systems and give Ukwuani people the opportunities they need to succeed.

How will Kedi do this?

  • Educate youths & women: Provide learning materials, scholarships and other education support initiatives alongside path to employment for outstanding scholars
  • Create job opportunities: Both home and abroad for youths and women by partnering with private and public organizations. Encourage and support early stage startups founded by Ukwuani youths with focus on tech and agriculture.
  • Empower our women: Attract and strengthen women support groups to increase trade and growth opportunities for women including but not limited to business loans and funding, mentorship opportunities, etc
